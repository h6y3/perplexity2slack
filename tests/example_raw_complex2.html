The First Ballerina Reactions Are All Saying The Same Thing (And There's A Reason For That)

Critics are mostly positive about Ballerina, praising its action and Ana de Armas' performance, but noting its shallow story 13. The film takes place between John Wick: Chapter 3 Parabellum and John Wick: Chapter 4 and includes appearances from Keanu Reeves and other actors from the main series 1.

Here is a summary of critical opinions:

  • Action and Choreography Many critics highlight the film's "brutal and relentless" action, with "mind-blowing fight scenes, phenomenally choreographed and staged"1. The action is considered intense and inventive, with a variety of weapons and creative sequences 14.

  • Ana de Armas' Performance De Armas is praised for her performance as a "true action star," with critics noting she "lights up the screen with deadly elegance and stunning choreography"1.

  • Story Some critics found the story to be "a little shallow" and "painfully boring"1. Others describe it as a "simple revenge story" 1.

  • John Wick elements Some critics feel that the inclusion of John Wick hurts the movie 1. Others state that the movie expands the John Wick universe and is a worthy addition to the franchise 14.

Key quotes from critics:

  • Jonathan Sim, ComingSoon.net: "Absolute mayhem from start to finish"1.

  • Chris Killian, ComicBook.com: "If all you want is to see Ana de Armas kill enough assassins to fill a small European village, then Ballerina is a bloody badass good time"1.

  • Germain Lussier, io9.com: "Ballerina is so hilariously over the top, I laughed through the whole thing, for better and worse...John Wick's inclusion hurts the movie too, which sucks, but Ana de Armas is a true action star"1.

  • Sean Tajipour, Nerdtropolis: "Ballerina is everything I hoped for. It is brutal, beautiful, and bursting with emotion"1.

  • Nadya Martinez, GothamGeekGirl: "Ballerina expands the world of John Wick with absolutely stunning backdrops, an exciting and gripping vengeful story of consequences, and a heavy arsenal of creative weapon choices and quick-witted resourcefulness"1.
